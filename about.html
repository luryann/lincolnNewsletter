Editorial Policy

The Railsplitter is an open forum for student expression at Lincoln High School. Editorials represent the views of the editorial board. All other opinions are those of individual student writers and do not represent the position of Lincoln High School, LAUSD, or the Railsplitter staff.

Letters to the editor and corrections may be submitted via the contact information below. The Railsplitter is committed to accuracy and fairness and will promptly correct any factual errors brought to our attention.
